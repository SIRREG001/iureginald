HealthVault Application: An Electronic Health Record Software

Project information

  • Category: Wireframe Development
  • Client: ALX_Africa
  • Project date:11 January, 2024
  • Project URL: HealthVault Pitch Deck

The traditional method of file documentation system affects the people of Africa, as they face the risk of data loss, data privacy compromise, and at worse death when their conditions need them to be moved to another hospital, the time wastage in opening a new medical record and not giving immediate medical attention can lead to loss of life. Addressing this challenge requires the development and deployment of a cloud-based, user-friendly, secured database management system specific to healthcare, where health information, medical records, and research can be stored for easy and fast diagnosis saving millions of lives, hence the solution we bring, The HealthVault 509.
